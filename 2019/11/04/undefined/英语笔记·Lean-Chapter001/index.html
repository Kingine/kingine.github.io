Leaning English Chapter 001

1.education n.教育 :vt.educate adj.educated :receive higher education 接受高等教育
2.overseas adj.海外的&adv.在海外地 :an overseas student 留学生 :study overseas = study abroad 出国留学
3.academic adj.学术的 :academic performance 学业表现
4.diploma n.文凭 :a high school diploma holder 高中文凭
5.degree n.学位 :a bachelor’s degree 学士学位 :a master’s degree 硕士学位 :a doctorate degree 博士学位

eg. a harvard-educated president
exp 一位在哈佛受过教育的总统

eg. Jack is an educated person always speaking in an articulate way.
exp 杰克是一个受过教育的人，说话口齿总是很清晰。

eg. Receiving an education is bene ficial to a person’s success.
exp 受教育对一个人的成功是有益的。

eg. I studied overseas at the age of 17 and returned last years.
exp 我17岁时出国留学，去年回国。

eg. Tom’s academic performance didn’t live up to his father’s expectations.
exp 汤姆在校的学业表现并未达到父亲的期许。

eg. * live up to sb’s expectations
exp 达到某人的期许

eg. I hold a diploma in management.
exp 我有管理学的文凭。

eg. I hold a bachelor’s degree in physics from Oxford University.
exp 我获得了牛津大学的物理学学士学位。
